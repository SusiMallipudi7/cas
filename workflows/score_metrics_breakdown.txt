  and the count of checked Knowledge Dependencies in engine.py (lines 68-82).

- Operational Complexity: 
  Evaluated dynamically based on Target Scope in engine.py (lines 85-93).

- Overall Risk Score & Autonomy Zone: 
  Calculated dynamically using the combined inputs against the engine's formula and decision matrix in engine.py (lines 47-125).
